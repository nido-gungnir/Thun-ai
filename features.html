A Feature for Every Stage of Farming

Thunai is more than an app; it's a complete support system for your farm.

Thunai Assistant (துணை உதவியாளர்)

The heart of our app. Simply ask questions in natural Tamil using your voice. Whether you need market prices, weather updates, or pest solutions, the Thunai Assistant understands and provides instant answers.

Crop Doctor (பயிர் மருத்துவர்)

Protect your crops from harm. Take a photo of an affected plant, and our powerful AI will instantly identify the pest or disease with high accuracy and suggest both organic and chemical treatment plans.

Market Prices (சந்தை நிலவரம்)

Never sell for less again. Get real-time price updates from over 15 markets across Tamil Nadu. Track price trends to make informed decisions on when and where to sell your harvest for the maximum profit.
