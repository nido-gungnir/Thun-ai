Empowering the Farmers of Tamil Nadu

We believe technology can bridge the information gap and create a more profitable and sustainable future for agriculture.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to put the power of data and technology into the hands of every small and marginal farmer. We aim to be the most trusted companion for farmers, providing them with the tools and knowledge they need to overcome challenges, reduce risks, and increase their income. We are dedicated to building a solution that is accessible, affordable, and truly serves the needs of the agricultural community.

Meet the Team

We are a passionate team of students from Coimbatore, combining our skills in technology and our respect for agriculture to build Thunai.
